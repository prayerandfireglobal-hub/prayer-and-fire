Prayer & Fire Ministry

Prayer & Fire is an online Christian ministry dedicated to prayer, spiritual renewal, biblical teaching, and encouraging believers around the world to grow in faith.

Our mission is to ignite hearts through prayer and the Word of God, fostering a global faith community rooted in truth, love, and spiritual fire.

This ministry operates online and serves believers through digital platforms, prayer initiatives, and faith-based resources.
